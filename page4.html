Bookings

How do I sign up for classes?

1. Email us at howkstudiosbooking@dance.sg or text our Whatsapp number at +65(91234567)
2. Register with us as a member and choose our class credits top-up system.
3. Top-up a minimum of $20 (or any amount in tens - i.e. $30, $40, $50, etc.);
Top-up $100 to receive free additional $10 class credits.
4. Redeem the class(es) you wish to take. Class prices for members range from $8 - $13.
5. That's it! Now come and dance your heart out!
*Should you choose not to become a member, you can also purchase a single class at $16 - no discounts or promotions apply.


*Class credits are non-transferrable and cannot be withdrawn, but they do not expire!



How do I make payment?

We accept Paynow online. Be sure to take a screen shot and send it to our Whatsapp number +65(91234567) for faster verification process.
We also accept CASH / NETS / PAYNOW at our studio.
