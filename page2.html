Our Founder

Ho Weng Kin a.k.a "Hawk"

With a passion for dance since young, Founder Weng Kin has made himself known in the dance community at a young age of 12. He has since made himself known as "Hawk", with his initials having a similar pronounciation. The Hawk of Singapore's dance community has won multiple competitions, both locally and internationally, over the many years. While being an Associate Professor of Mathematics & Mathematics Education at the National Institute of Education, he has been able to continuously exude his passion in both teaching Mathematics and Dance. He thus set up HOWK Studios as a means to share his passion and experience in Dance with people of all ages and dance experience. He is now happily married with 2 daughters and 1 son.



Hawk has been trained in many styles of dance over his years of being a dancer. From breakdancing to popping, his abilities as a dancer with both talent and hard work has given him many opportunities to compete in the global dance scene. It is now his greatest dream as a dancer to share the many experiences and skills he has with those that have a passion for dance. Currently, he is teaching HipHop Classes here at HOWK Studios.
