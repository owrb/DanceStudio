What are open classes?

Open classes are independent unique dance classes focused on choreography. You will experience learning a routine within 1 hour. Our instructors teach a different routine every week.
Open classes are open to all regardless of age, dance experience or nationality. Do come down to give it a try!


Class Levels

Intro Level- Introductory classes for beginners to experience a simple and short dance routine focusing on basic moves. If you have just started learning dance or have never danced before, our intro classes are a great way to learn!

Level 1- Suitable for beginner dancers with some dance foundation. Get to experience learning slightly longer and difficult choreography than what we offer in our intro level classes. The only way to improve is to keep dancing!

Level 2- Suitable for experienced dancers. Challenge yourselves with more complex and advanced choreography, pushing you to exceed your limits in dance!

What songs do you use for open classes?

As each open class has its own unique choreography, the songs we use will change for every class! Follow our social media pages for weekly updates on the songs each instructor will be using!

You can also email us at howkstudios@dance.sg for song requests. We will try our best to accommodate to your wishes!
