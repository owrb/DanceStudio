Where is HOWK Studios?

We are located at Sunshine Plaza #03-01/02!

From the main entrance, walk all the way in to find the lift lobby.
Alight at 3rd floor, follow down the passageway and turn right.

By MRT:
Sunshine Plaza is close to the following MRT Stations:
Bencoolen (DT21), Dhoby Ghaut (CC1,NE6,NS24), Rochor (DT13) and Bugis (DT14,EW12).

By Bus:
Bus Services 56, 64, 65, 131, 131A, 139, 147, 147A, 166, 857 and 980 stop at bus stops near Sunshine Plaza.
